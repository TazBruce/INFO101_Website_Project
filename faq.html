Frequently Asked Questions

How long is a day tour and when do they start?

On average, day tours run for around 5-6 hours and begin at 11am.


What should I bring on the tour?

  • Water bottle
  • Windbreaker jacket
  • Warm clothes
  • Camera
  • Your booking reference number
  • Spare clothes if you're booking the adrenaline tour
  • Spare cash/card for food and souvenirs!

COVID-19?

At level-2 and below, Welly Wanders will be running tours all around the Wellington region at a reduced price! However, maximum group numbers will be reduced to comply with spacing rules.


Do you organise private tours?

Yes! Please create a query here to have a tour specialised for you and your group!


What if it's raining on my booked date?

We take rain into consideration while preparing tours so that you can enjoy our services on any occasion! However, if you you'd prefer the booking to be rescheduled for another day or refunded, please refer to the refunds section.


Do you cater for those with disabilities?

Yes! Please create a query here to have a tour specialised for you and your group's needs!


Do you cater food?

At this current time, we do not cater food during tours, however, breaks will occur during the tour to allow for people to buy lunch.


Can I get a refund?

We do accept refunds, however, our policy requires that cancellations must be made 7 days before the booked date to be eligible for a refund. This also applies for rescheduled dates. Please email us at support@WellyWanders.com for further queries.


How can I provide feedback or criticism?

Please email us at feedback@WellyWanders.com and we'll be sure to respond!
