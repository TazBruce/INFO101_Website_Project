About Us

Who Are We?

Welly Wanders is a 100%NZ owned and operated business focused on providing exciting and unforgettable tours that are suited to any group. These tours could vary from embracing the artistic aspects of Wellington or learning about the culture and history of the native maori in Aotearoa (New Zealand). However, if you and your group happen to prefer more adrenaline based activities or maybe even enjoying a mixture of everything, we can meet your needs!



Our Goal

We strive to offer environmentally sustainable tours in and around the Wellington region. It is our priority to maintain a high sustainability model which means all transport we provide must be carbon neutral. Whether it be electric cars, bikes, or even scooters, you can rest assured that we are working together towards a green future!
